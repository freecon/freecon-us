• Freecon

A meeting of free software enthusiasts.

October 2015

Noisebridge, 2169 Mission St., San Francisco, California


Freecon is a conference for Free Software enthusiasts in the San Francisco Bay area.

We're building an event for software developers, activists, and computer users to learn skills and discuss challenges to software freedom.

Things are just getting started, so subscribe to our email list to follow along as things develop.

If you'd like to get involved, check us out on #freecon in Freenode IRC
